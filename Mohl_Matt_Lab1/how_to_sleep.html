How to Properly Sleep

Phase 1: Getting Prepared

  1. Gather things required to wake up

    This could be any of the following

    • a phone
    • watch
    • or anything with an alarm on it

  2. Gather any chargers

    As with all electronics, you will need to charge them for use the next day. Make sure to grab any media chargers necessary to do so.

Phase 2: Getting to the bedroom

  1. Locate your area of slumber

    Where will you sleep? Answer this question, then go to that location.

  2. Make yourself comfortable

    Lie down and relax. Maybe listen to some music, watch some TV, or just count ceiling tiles.

  3. Close eyes and sleep
