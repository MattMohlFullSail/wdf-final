Validation Check

Google.com

Google.com did not validate for HTML 2.1. The homepage generated about 29 errors, most of them caused by use of obsolete attributes such as border, cellpadding, and bgcolor. They could fix this by actually using CSS to style their content, instead of outdated HTML attributes.

YouTube.com

YouTube.com did not validate for HTML 2.1, generating 84 errors. I couldn't understand any specific errors, but from what I saw, the main cause was use of HTML for styling as opposed to CSS.

Twitter.com

Twitter.com was extremely close to validating as HTML 2.1, but fell two errors short. I can't for the life of me figure out what they mean, so I'm pretty sure they are validator errors.

stumbleupon.com

stumbleupon.com also came close to validating as HTML 2.1. They ended up with 11 errors, however, most of them generating because they neglected to include alt attributes inside their img elements.
